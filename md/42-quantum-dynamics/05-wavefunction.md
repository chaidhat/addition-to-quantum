# Wavefunction

Where $\left\lvert \psi(t) \right\rangle$ is a [<span class="underline">state</span>](#ket-states) in the [<span class="underline">Hilbert space</span>](#hilbert-spaces), $\psi(x,t)$ is the wavefunction w.r.t time $t$ and position $x$.

Let
$$\psi(x,t)\triangleq\left\langle x|\psi(t) \right\rangle$$
