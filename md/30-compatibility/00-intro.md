# Compatibility

Compatibility is a property of two [<span class="underline">operators</span>](#operators).

If $\left\lvert \phi_n \right\rangle$ is the $n$-th [<span class="underline">eigenstate</span>](#eigenstates) of $\hat{A}$ (i.e., the [<span class="underline">eigenbasis</span>](#eigenbasis) of $\hat{A}$),

Let
$$\hat{A}=\sum_{n=1}^N a_n\left\lvert \phi_n \right\rangle\left\langle \phi_n \right\rvert$$
If $\hat{B}$ is compatible with $\hat{A}$ (i.e., $\hat{B}$ is diagonalizable with $\hat{A}$) then,
$$\hat{B}=\sum_{n=1}^N b_n\left\lvert \phi_n \right\rangle\left\langle \phi_n \right\rvert$$
