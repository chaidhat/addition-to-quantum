# Spin-1/2 System

This spin is for 2-dimensional [[hilbert-spaces]].
Electrons, protons, neutrons and quarks have spin-1/2.
For spin-1/2 systems,
$$S_x\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\sigma_x\quad
    S_y\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\sigma_y\quad
    S_z\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\sigma_z$$
i.e.,
$$S_x\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\begin{pmatrix}0 & 1\\ 1 & 0\end{pmatrix}\quad
    S_y\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\begin{pmatrix}0 & -i\\ i & 0\end{pmatrix}\quad
    S_z\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\begin{pmatrix}1 & 0\\ 0 & -1\end{pmatrix}$$
Note that
$$\left\lvert z+ \right\rangle=\left\lvert \uparrow \right\rangle = \left\lvert 0 \right\rangle=\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ 0\end{pmatrix}\quad
    \left\lvert z- \right\rangle=\left\lvert \downarrow \right\rangle = \left\lvert 1 \right\rangle=\begin{pmatrix}0 \\ 1\end{pmatrix}$$
And
$$\left\lvert x+ \right\rangle=\left\lvert + \right\rangle=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\left\lvert 0 \right\rangle+\left\lvert 1 \right\rangle)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ 1\end{pmatrix}\quad
    \left\lvert x- \right\rangle=\left\lvert - \right\rangle=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(\left\lvert 0 \right\rangle-\left\lvert 1 \right\rangle)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ -1\end{pmatrix}$$
$$|y+\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\big(|z+\rangle + i|z-\rangle\big) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ i \end{pmatrix}, \qquad |y-\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\big(|z+\rangle - i|z-\rangle\big) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ -i \end{pmatrix}$$
