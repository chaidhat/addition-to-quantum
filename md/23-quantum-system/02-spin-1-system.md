# Spin-1 System

This spin is for 3-dimensional [<span class="underline">Hilbert spaces</span>](#hilbert-spaces).
W/Z bosons, photons and gluons have spin-1.
For spin-1 systems,
$$S_x\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\Sigma_x\quad
    S_y\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\Sigma_y\quad
    S_z\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{2}\Sigma_z$$
i.e.,
$$S_x\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}0 & 1 & 0\\ 1 & 0 & 1\\ 0 & 1 & 0\end{pmatrix}\quad
    S_y\triangleq\frac{\hbar}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix}0 & -i & 0\\ i & 0 & -i\\ 0 & i & 0\end{pmatrix}\quad
    S_z\triangleq\hbar\begin{pmatrix}1 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & -1\end{pmatrix}$$
Note that
$$\left\lvert 1 \right\rangle=\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ 0 \\ 0\end{pmatrix}\quad
    \left\lvert 0 \right\rangle=\begin{pmatrix}0 \\ 1 \\ 0\end{pmatrix}\quad
    \left\lvert -1 \right\rangle=\begin{pmatrix}0 \\ 0 \\ -1\end{pmatrix}\quad$$
For photons, $S_z$ is often called rectilinear and $S_x$ is often called diagonal and $S_y$ are often called circular.
