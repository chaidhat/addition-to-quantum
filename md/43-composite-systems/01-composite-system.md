# Composite System

A [<span class="underline">quantum system</span>](#quantum-system) made up of two or more sub quantum systems.
Let $H_A$ and $H_B$ be two state spaces. The state space of the composite system is
$$H_{AB} = H_A\otimes H_B$$

It is represented by a [<span class="underline">tensor product space</span>](#tensor-product-space).
