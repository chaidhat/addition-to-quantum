# Bell States

For reference, Bell defined these as
$$\begin{aligned}
        \left\lvert \Phi^+ \right\rangle & = \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\left\lvert 00 \right\rangle + \left\lvert 11 \right\rangle\right) \\
        \left\lvert \Phi^- \right\rangle & = \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\left\lvert 00 \right\rangle - \left\lvert 11 \right\rangle\right) \\
        \left\lvert \Psi^+ \right\rangle & = \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\left\lvert 01 \right\rangle + \left\lvert 10 \right\rangle\right) \\
        \left\lvert \Psi^- \right\rangle & = \tfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\left(\left\lvert 01 \right\rangle - \left\lvert 10 \right\rangle\right)
    \end{aligned}$$
