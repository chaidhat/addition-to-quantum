# Complex Number Trigonometry

$$\cos\theta = \frac{e^{i\theta} + e^{-i\theta}}{2}$$
$$\sin\theta = \frac{e^{i\theta} - e^{-i\theta}}{2i}$$
$$\tan\theta = \frac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta} = \frac{1}{i}\cdot\frac{e^{i\theta} - e^{-i\theta}}{e^{i\theta} + e^{-i\theta}} = -i\cdot\frac{e^{i\theta} - e^{-i\theta}}{e^{i\theta} + e^{-i\theta}}$$
