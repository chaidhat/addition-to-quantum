# Conjugate

Say
$$z=a+bi$$

Let the conjugate $z^*$ of $z$ be defined as this (we just
make the imaginary part negative)

$$\begin{gather*}
    z^*=a-bi=\begin{pmatrix}a  & +b \\
                   -b & a
    \end{pmatrix}
\end{gather*}$$

If you’re writing in [[euler-form]], then flip the sign of the angle
$$(e^{i\theta})^*=e^{-i\theta}$$
