# Hilbert Space

A Hilbert space $H$ describes quantum space.

$$\begin{gather*}
    H
\end{gather*}$$

A Hilbert space is a [<span class="underline">vector space</span>](#vector-spaces), possibly infinite-dimensional.

$H$ has an [<span class="underline">inner product</span>](#inner-product) and is complete.

The states we wrote before for example $\left\lvert \psi \right\rangle$ are [<span class="underline">states</span>](#states) which
exist in a Hilbert space.
