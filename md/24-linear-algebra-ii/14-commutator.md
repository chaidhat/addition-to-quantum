# Commutator

The commutator of two [<span class="underline">operators</span>](#operators) $\hat{A},\hat{B}$ is defined as

$$\begin{gather*}
    [\hat{A},\hat{B}]=\hat{A}\hat{B}-\hat{B}\hat{A}
\end{gather*}$$

If $[\hat{A},\hat{B}]=0$ then that means you can commute
[<span class="underline">matrix
multiplication</span>](#bra-ket-matrix-multiplication).

If "$\hat{A}$ commutes with $\hat{B}$", then that means $\hat{A}\hat{B}=\hat{B}\hat{A}$ which also means $[\hat{A},\hat{B}]=0$.
