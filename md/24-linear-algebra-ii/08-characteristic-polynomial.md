# Characteristic Polynomial

polynomial
$p(\lambda)=\alpha_0\lambda^n+\alpha_1\lambda^{n-1}+...+\alpha_{n-1}\lambda^{1}+\alpha_n$

$$\begin{gather*}
    p(\lambda)=det(A-\lambda I)
\end{gather*}$$

Solving

$$\begin{gather*}
    p(\lambda)=0
\end{gather*}$$

Gives $\lambda$ values which are [<span class="underline">eigenvalues</span>](#eigenvectorseigenvalues) of A
