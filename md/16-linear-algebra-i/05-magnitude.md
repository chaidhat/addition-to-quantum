# Magnitude

Where $n$ is the dimensions, the magnitude of $\left\lvert \psi \right\rangle$ is
defined as:

$$\begin{gather*}
    ||\left\lvert \psi \right\rangle||=||\begin{pmatrix}\psi_1\\\psi_2\\\vdots\\\psi_n\end{pmatrix}||=\sqrt{|\psi_1|^2+|\psi_2|^2+...+|\psi_n|^2}
\end{gather*}$$

Note that
$$||\left\lvert \psi \right\rangle||^2=\left\langle \psi|\psi \right\rangle$$
You’ll know what a bra-ket is later.
