# Orthogonality

State $\left\lvert u \right\rangle,\left\lvert v \right\rangle$ are orthogonal to each other if the
[<span class="underline">inner
product</span>](#inner-product) of state $\left\lvert u \right\rangle$ and $\left\lvert v \right\rangle$ are zero.

$$\begin{gather*}
    \left\lvert u \right\rangle\cdot \left\lvert v \right\rangle=0
\end{gather*}$$

They’re also known as being perpendicular!
