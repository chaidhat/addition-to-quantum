# Inner product I

Inner product of states $\left\lvert u \right\rangle$ and $\left\lvert v \right\rangle$ is defined as

$$\begin{gather*}
    \left\lvert u \right\rangle\cdot \left\lvert v \right\rangle=u_1^*v_1+u_2^*v_2+...+u_n^*v_n
\end{gather*}$$

(if the entries are real numbers, the [<span class="underline">conjugates</span>](#conjugate) do nothing and this
reduces to the usual dot product.)
