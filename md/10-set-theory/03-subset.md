# Subset

[<span class="underline">sets</span>](#set)
can be subsets (or equal) of another set if all [<span class="underline">elements</span>](#element) of a
[<span class="underline">set</span>](#set)
is in another
[<span class="underline">set</span>](#set).

Example:

$$\begin{gather*}
    S\subset\{3,5,1982549,67\}
\end{gather*}$$

We have a line underneath to show it could be a subset OR equal

$$\begin{gather*}
    S\subseteq\{3,5,1982549\}
\end{gather*}$$
