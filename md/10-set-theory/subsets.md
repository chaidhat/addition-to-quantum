# Subset

[[set|sets]]
can be subsets (or equal) of another set if all [[element|elements]] of a
[[set]]
is in another
[[set]].

Example:

$$\begin{gather*}
    S\subset\{3,5,1982549,67\}
\end{gather*}$$

We have a line underneath to show it could be a subset OR equal

$$\begin{gather*}
    S\subseteq\{3,5,1982549\}
\end{gather*}$$
